How "Live" should the Expert participation be?

If the Router pulls in an Expert (e.g., the Project Manager) during the call, how should they contribute?

A

The "Whisperer" (Back-channel)

The Expert sends "pushed context" to the Router. The Router then says: "Wait, the PM is reminding me that we already have a task for that in the Roadmap. Is this different?"

B

The "Interjection" (Multi-voice)

The Expert speaks directly to you with a different voice/identity: "I'm stepping in here as the Architect—if we build it that way, it will break the Sync goal we set yesterday."

C

The "Reviewer" (Post-Call Only)

The conversation stays 1-on-1 with the Router. After you hang up, the Experts review the transcript and send you a notification/summary with their findings.
