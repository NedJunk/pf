Voice Interaction Model

A speech-based conversational assistant for task untangling.

How do you envision the audio interaction working?

To build the right voice experience, how should the tool listen and respond?

A

Voice-In, Text-Out (Dictation & Processing)

I speak my brain dump, and the tool processes it into a prioritized, readable text list that I can review and check off.

B

Two-Way Voice (Smart Speaker Style)

A back-and-forth verbal conversation where the assistant speaks back to me, asking clarifying questions to help me prioritize.

C

Voice Command Driven

Short, specific commands ("Add task", "What's next?", "Snooze this") rather than long conversational brainstorming.
