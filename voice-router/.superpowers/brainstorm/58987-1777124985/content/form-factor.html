Interface & Workflow

Tackling task overwhelm requires the right environment.

What type of interface works best for your brain when you are overwhelmed?

Since this is built specifically for *you*, how do you want to interact with this tool?

A

Minimalist CLI / Terminal

Keyboard-driven, text-only, invisible until I need it. I want to stay in my dev environment and avoid visual noise.

B

Visual Web Dashboard

Kanban boards, drag-and-drop prioritization, color-coding. I need to *see* the big picture to make sense of it.

C

Conversational Assistant

A chat interface where I dump my brain, and it helps me negotiate what to do next based on my current state.

D

Menu Bar / System Tray App

Always visible but out of the way. Focuses on the *one* thing I should be doing right now, with quick drop-downs.
